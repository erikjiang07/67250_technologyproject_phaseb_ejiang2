Welcome to MonoMuse

Explore the future of clothing, wearable tech, and textile innovation.

Plan Your Visit

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum in Pittsburgh exploring the intersection of fashion, wearable technology, and textile innovation.

From smart fabrics and 3D-printed garments to AI-generated design and digital fashion, MonoMuse showcases how technology is transforming the way we dress, create, and express ourselves. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the future of fashion. Plan your visit to experience immersive installations, hands-on workshops, and collections designed to inspire creativity at the edge of fashion and technology.
